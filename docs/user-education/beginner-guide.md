# RemoteDesk Beginner Guide

## Welcome!
RemoteDesk lets you access and control computers remotely.

## Getting Started (5 minutes)

### 1. Create Your Account
- Go to https://remotedesk.io
- Click "Sign Up"
- Enter your email and create a password
- Verify your email

### 2. Install RemoteDesk
- Download the desktop app for your OS
- Or use the web app directly
- Log in with your account

### 3. Your Desk ID
- Every user gets a unique 9-digit Desk ID
- Example: 123-456-789
- Share this with people who want to connect

### 4. Connect to Someone
- Ask for their Desk ID
- Enter it in the "Connect" field
- Wait for them to accept
- You are now connected!

### 5. Let Someone Connect to You
- Share your Desk ID
- When they connect, you will see a request
- Click "Accept" to allow
- They can now see your screen

## Key Features
- Screen sharing with HD quality
- Remote control (mouse and keyboard)
- File transfer
- Clipboard sync
- Chat during sessions

## Tips
- Keep your Desk ID private
- Only accept connections from people you trust
- End session when done
- Use strong passwords
