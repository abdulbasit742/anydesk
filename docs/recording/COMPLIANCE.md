# Recording Compliance Notes

## GDPR Compliance
- Explicit consent required from both parties
- Recording indicator visible at all times
- Right to erasure - users can request deletion
- Data retention limits enforced automatically
- Data processing agreement available

## HIPAA Considerations
- Session recordings may contain PHI
- Access controls and audit logging required
- Encryption in transit and at rest
- Business Associate Agreement required

## SOC2 Type II
- All recording access is logged
- Tamper-evident audit trail
- Role-based access controls
- Regular access reviews
