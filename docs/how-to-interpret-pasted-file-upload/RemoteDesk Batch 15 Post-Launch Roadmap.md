# RemoteDesk Batch 15 Post-Launch Roadmap

## Introduction
This document outlines the strategic roadmap for RemoteDesk following the enterprise launch, focusing on the continued evolution and improvement of features initiated in Batch 15. It provides a vision for the next 12-18 months, ensuring alignment with user needs and business objectives.

## Strategic Themes

1.  **Continuous Improvement of Enterprise Features:** Refining and expanding managed deployment, SSO, SCIM, and session policies based on user feedback and market trends.
2.  **Advanced Security and Compliance:** Enhancing DLP capabilities, automating compliance evidence collection, and strengthening overall system security.
3.  **Ecosystem and Integration Growth:** Expanding the marketplace, productizing more APIs, and deepening integrations with key third-party platforms.
4.  **Mobile and Fleet Excellence:** Delivering a feature-rich mobile experience and advanced fleet management capabilities.
5.  **Operational Excellence and Observability:** Improving incident management, enhancing system observability, and optimizing support and customer success operations.
6.  **AI-Powered Insights and Automation:** Leveraging AI for intelligent troubleshooting, predictive analytics, and automated task execution.

## Roadmap Milestones

### Quarter 3, 2026
- **Managed Deployment:** Advanced deployment automation and multi-cloud support.
- **SSO:** Expanded Identity Provider (IdP) integrations and adaptive authentication.
- **Device Enrollment:** Zero-touch enrollment and advanced policy assignment.
- **Compliance:** Automated evidence collection and audit trail reporting.
- **API Productization:** Advanced API gateway and developer analytics.
- **Observability:** Advanced correlation and predictive analytics.

### Quarter 4, 2026
- **SCIM:** Full SCIM 2.0 compliance and attribute mapping.
- **Session Policies:** Context-aware policies and AI-driven anomaly detection.
- **Support Automation:** AI-powered ticket routing and self-service portal integration.
- **Marketplace:** Developer SDKs and revenue sharing models.
- **Fleet Management:** Remote scripting and patch management integration.
- **Post-Launch Ops:** Automated incident response and root cause analysis.

### Quarter 1, 2027
- **DLP:** Real-time content inspection and custom policy engine.
- **Customer Success:** Predictive churn analysis and personalized engagement.
- **Mobile Roadmap:** Feature parity with desktop and offline capabilities.
- **AI Integration:** Initial AI-powered troubleshooting and assistance features.

## Conclusion

This post-launch roadmap will be regularly reviewed and updated to reflect changes in priorities, technology, and market conditions. Our commitment remains focused on providing a world-class remote work solution that empowers our users and meets the evolving needs of enterprise organizations.
