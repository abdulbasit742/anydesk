# RemoteDesk Partner Onboarding Guide

## Introduction
This guide outlines the step-by-step process for onboarding new partners to the RemoteDesk Partner Program. A structured onboarding process ensures that partners are equipped with the necessary knowledge, tools, and resources to successfully represent and sell RemoteDesk solutions, fostering a strong and productive partnership.

## Onboarding Phases

### Phase 1: Application & Approval

1.  **Partner Application Submission:** Prospective partners complete and submit the online RemoteDesk Partner Application form.
    -   *Action:* Partner
    -   *Resource:* [Link to Partner Application Form]
2.  **Initial Review by RemoteDesk Partner Team:** The RemoteDesk Partner Team reviews the application for completeness and initial fit.
    -   *Action:* RemoteDesk Partner Team
3.  **Interview & Qualification:** A RemoteDesk Partner Manager conducts an interview with the prospective partner to discuss business models, market reach, and alignment.
    -   *Action:* RemoteDesk Partner Team, Partner
4.  **Due Diligence & Background Check:** RemoteDesk performs necessary due diligence, including background and reference checks.
    -   *Action:* RemoteDesk Partner Team
5.  **Approval Notification:** The partner receives an official approval notification from RemoteDesk.
    -   *Action:* RemoteDesk Partner Team

### Phase 2: Legal & Administrative Setup

1.  **Partner Agreement Signing:** The approved partner signs the RemoteDesk Partner Agreement.
    -   *Action:* Partner, RemoteDesk Legal
    -   *Resource:* Partner Agreement Document
2.  **Account Creation:** RemoteDesk creates the partner account in the Partner Portal and internal systems.
    -   *Action:* RemoteDesk Operations
3.  **Access to Partner Portal:** Partner receives login credentials and instructions for accessing the RemoteDesk Partner Portal.
    -   *Action:* RemoteDesk Operations
    -   *Resource:* [Link to Partner Portal]
4.  **Tax & Banking Information:** Partner submits required tax and banking information for commission payouts.
    -   *Action:* Partner
    -   *Resource:* Secure Upload Portal for Financial Information

### Phase 3: Training & Certification

1.  **Product Training:** Partners undergo comprehensive training on RemoteDesk products, features, and use cases.
    -   *Action:* Partner
    -   *Resource:* RemoteDesk Partner Training Modules (online courses, webinars)
2.  **Sales Training:** Training on RemoteDesk’s sales methodology, competitive positioning, and pricing models.
    -   *Action:* Partner
    -   *Resource:* RemoteDesk Sales Enablement Kit
3.  **Technical Training (for Integration/Technology Partners):** In-depth technical training on APIs, SDKs, and integration best practices.
    -   *Action:* Partner
    -   *Resource:* RemoteDesk Developer Documentation, API Sandboxes
4.  **Certification Exam:** Partners complete a certification exam to demonstrate proficiency.
    -   *Action:* Partner
    -   *Resource:* Online Certification Platform
5.  **Certification Awarded:** Upon successful completion, partners receive their official RemoteDesk Partner Certification.
    -   *Action:* RemoteDesk Partner Team

### Phase 4: Go-to-Market & Launch

1.  **Marketing Collateral Access:** Partners gain access to co-brandable marketing materials, logos, and brand guidelines.
    -   *Action:* Partner
    -   *Resource:* Partner Marketing Resource Library
2.  **Sales Enablement Kit:** Partners receive a sales enablement kit including presentations, battle cards, and demo scripts.
    -   *Action:* Partner
    -   *Resource:* Partner Sales Resource Library
3.  **Joint Marketing Planning (Optional):** Collaborative planning for joint marketing activities (e.g., webinars, case studies, press releases).
    -   *Action:* RemoteDesk Marketing, Partner Marketing
4.  **Launch Announcement:** Official announcement of the new partnership (if applicable).
    -   *Action:* RemoteDesk Marketing, Partner Marketing
5.  **Dedicated Partner Manager Assignment:** A dedicated RemoteDesk Partner Manager is assigned to provide ongoing support and guidance.
    -   *Action:* RemoteDesk Partner Team

## Partner Portal Overview

The RemoteDesk Partner Portal is the central hub for all partner activities, providing access to:
-   **Deal Registration:** Submit and track new leads and opportunities.
-   **Training & Certification:** Access training modules and track certification progress.
-   **Marketing Resources:** Download marketing collateral, logos, and brand guidelines.
-   **Sales Resources:** Access sales presentations, pricing, and demo environments.
-   **Commission Reports:** View and track commission payouts.
-   **Support:** Access partner-specific support resources and contact information.
