# Recommended Team Structure

## Core Team (8-10 people)
- 2 Backend Engineers (API, signaling)
- 2 Frontend Engineers (web, desktop)
- 1 DevOps/SRE
- 1 Security Engineer
- 1 Product Manager
- 1 Designer
- 1 QA Engineer
- 1 Technical Writer

## Extended Team
- Customer Success
- Sales
- Support
- Legal/Compliance
