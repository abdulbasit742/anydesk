# RemoteDesk Partner Branding Guidelines

## Logo Usage
- Use approved RemoteDesk logo from Partner Portal
- Maintain aspect ratio; never stretch or distort
- Minimum clear space: 2x logo height on all sides
- Approved color versions: Full color, white, black

## Color Palette
| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Primary | #0A2540 | Headers, key elements |
| Accent | #00D4AA | CTAs, highlights |
| Neutral | #6B7280 | Body text |
| Light | #F6F9FC | Backgrounds |

## Typography
- Headlines: Inter Bold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Code: JetBrains Mono

## Co-branded Materials
- RemoteDesk logo must appear equal or larger than partner logo
- Include "Partner of RemoteDesk" tagline
- Link to remotedesk.io on all digital materials

## Prohibited Uses
- Do not modify logo colors outside approved palette
- Do not use logo on backgrounds with < 4.5:1 contrast
- Do not imply endorsement beyond partnership level
- Do not use RemoteDesk trademarks in domain names

## Review Process
Submit co-branded materials to brand@remotedesk.io
Typical review turnaround: 2 business days
