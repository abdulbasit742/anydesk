# RemoteDesk 80-File Feature Pack - Risk Register

This risk register identifies potential risks associated with the integration and deployment of the 80-file feature pack for RemoteDesk, along with their potential impact, likelihood, and proposed mitigation strategies.

## 1. Risk Categories

*   **Technical Risks**: Related to software architecture, design, implementation, and integration.
*   **Operational Risks**: Related to deployment, monitoring, and ongoing maintenance.
*   **Security Risks**: Related to vulnerabilities, data breaches, and compliance.
*   **Performance Risks**: Related to application responsiveness, scalability, and resource utilization.
*   **Project Management Risks**: Related to timelines, resources, and scope.
