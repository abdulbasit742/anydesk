# Admin Billing Guide

## Subscription Management

### Viewing Your Plan
- Admin Dashboard -> Billing
- See current plan, seats, cost
- View usage vs. allocation

### Changing Plans
| From | To | Effect |
|------|-----|--------|
| Free | Pro | Immediate, prorated charge |
| Pro | Enterprise | Contact sales |
| Pro | Free | End of billing period |
| Annual | Monthly | End of annual term |

### Managing Seats
- Add seats: Immediate charge for prorated amount
- Remove seats: Credit applied to next invoice
- Reassign seats: No charge
- Minimum: 1 seat on paid plans

### Payment Methods
- Credit/Debit card
- ACH (US only)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise)
- PayPal (select regions)

### Invoices
- Download from Billing -> Invoices
- Sent to billing contact email
- Include VAT/GST if configured
- PO number supported (Enterprise)

## Usage Reports
- Monthly active users
- Session hours
- Feature adoption
- License utilization
- Export as CSV/PDF

## Cancellation
1. Admin Dashboard -> Billing
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Access continues until period end
5. Data export available for 30 days

## Refunds
- Pro-rated within 30 days
- Contact billing@remotedesk.io
- Processing: 5-10 business days
