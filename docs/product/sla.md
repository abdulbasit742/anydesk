# Service Level Agreement

## Uptime
- **Target**: 99.9% monthly uptime
- **Measurement**: API availability via `/health`
- **Exclusions**: Scheduled maintenance, force majeure

## Response Times
- **API**: P95 < 200ms
- **WebSocket**: P95 < 50ms
- **WebRTC**: Initial connection < 5s

## Support Response
- **Enterprise**: 1 hour (24/7)
- **Pro**: 4 hours (business hours)
- **Free**: Best effort

## Data
- **Backup**: Daily automated backups
- **Retention**: 30 days point-in-time recovery
- **RPO**: 1 hour
- **RTO**: 4 hours

## Scheduled Maintenance
- **Window**: Sundays 2-4 AM UTC
- **Notice**: 72 hours minimum
- **Duration**: < 2 hours

## Credits
- < 99.9%: 10% credit
- < 99%: 25% credit
- < 95%: 50% credit

## Escalation
1. Email: support@remotedesk.io
2. Emergency: +1-800-REMOTEDESK
3. Status: status.remotedesk.io
