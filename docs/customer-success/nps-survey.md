# NPS Survey

## Questions
1. How likely are you to recommend RemoteDesk? (0-10)
2. What is the primary reason for your score?
3. What could we do better?

## Timing
- 30 days after signup
- After 10 sessions
- Quarterly for active users
- Before renewal

## Follow-up
- Promoters (9-10): Request review/testimonial
- Passives (7-8): Identify improvement areas
- Detractors (0-6): Immediate outreach
