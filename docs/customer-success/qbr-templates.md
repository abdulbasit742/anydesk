# Quarterly Business Review Templates

## Pre-QBR Checklist
- [ ] Usage data pulled
- [ ] Support ticket analysis complete
- [ ] Feature adoption metrics ready
- [ ] Customer health score calculated
- [ ] Success plan progress reviewed
- [ ] Expansion opportunities identified
- [ ] Attendees confirmed
- [ ] Agenda shared 48h in advance

## QBR Agenda Template
```
1. Welcome & Introductions (5 min)
2. RemoteDesk Product Updates (10 min)
3. Usage & Adoption Review (15 min)
4. Goals & Success Plan Progress (15 min)
5. Support & Satisfaction Review (10 min)
6. Expansion Opportunities (10 min)
7. Q3 Goals & Action Items (10 min)
8. Open Discussion (10 min)
Total: 85 minutes
```

## QBR Slide Deck Outline
1. Title slide (customer logo + RemoteDesk logo)
2. Executive Summary
3. Usage Highlights
4. Feature Adoption Deep Dive
5. Success Plan Progress
6. Support Metrics
7. What's New in RemoteDesk
8. Recommended Next Steps
9. Q&A

## Follow-up
Send summary within 24 hours including:
- Action items with owners
- Agreed goals for next quarter
- Resources shared
- Next meeting scheduled
