# RemoteDesk Churn Prevention

## Early Warning Signs
| Signal | Threshold | Action |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Login frequency drop | < 50% of baseline | Outreach within 48h |
| Feature usage decline | < 30% of peak | Training offer |
| Support ticket increase | > 5 tickets/month | Escalation path |
| NPS score drop | < 6 | Executive call |
| License underutilization | < 50% seats active | Right-sizing discussion |
| No engagement | No login in 30 days | Rescue campaign |

## Churn Risk Tiers
| Tier | Criteria | Intervention |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Green | Score 80-100 | Maintain relationship |
| Yellow | Score 60-79 | Proactive check-in |
| Red | Score 40-59 | Intensive engagement |
| Critical | Score 0-39 | Executive rescue |

## Rescue Playbook
1. Understand root cause (survey/interview)
2. Address specific pain points
3. Offer training/consulting
4. Temporary discount if needed
5. Executive commitment
6. 30-day check-in

## Exit Interview
If churn occurs:
1. Conduct exit interview
2. Document reasons
3. Share with product team
4. Maintain relationship
5. 6-month win-back campaign
