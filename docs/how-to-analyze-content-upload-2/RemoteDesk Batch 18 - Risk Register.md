# RemoteDesk Batch 18 - Risk Register

This document identifies potential risks associated with the features developed in RemoteDesk Batch 18, focusing on Advanced Features, Collaboration Tools, Mobile Readiness, and Enterprise Integration & Security. Each risk includes a description, category, likelihood, impact, severity, proposed mitigation strategies, and a suggested owner.

## Risk Categories
- **Technical**: Risks related to technology, architecture, performance, and scalability.
- **Security**: Risks related to data breaches, unauthorized access, and compliance.
- **Integration**: Risks associated with connecting to external systems or platforms.
- **User Experience (UX)**: Risks impacting user satisfaction, usability, and adoption.
- **Operational**: Risks related to deployment, monitoring, and support.

| R18-001 | **Performance degradation with multi-monitor streaming**: Streaming multiple high-resolution displays simultaneously may consume excessive bandwidth and CPU, leading to lag and poor user experience. | Technical | Medium | High | Critical | Implement adaptive bitrate streaming, region-of-interest encoding, and hardware acceleration. | R18-003 | **Audio synchronization issues during remote audio streaming**: Latency or dropped packets could cause audio to be out of sync with video, or lead to choppy audio quality. | Technical | Medium | Medium | Significant | Prioritize audio packets in network transmission. | R18-005 | **Large session recording file sizes impacting storage**: High-quality, long-duration session recordings could quickly consume local and cloud storage, leading to high costs and storage management overhead. | Operational | High | Medium | Significant | Implement configurable recording quality settings.
