# RemoteDesk Batch 17 Risk Register

This document identifies potential risks associated with the development and deployment of features covered in Batch 17 of the RemoteDesk SaaS project. It includes risks related to desktop distribution, auto-update, diagnostics, enterprise controls, and final artifacts.

## Overview
This risk register serves as a proactive measure to identify, assess, and plan mitigation strategies for potential issues that could impact the successful delivery, functionality, security, or maintainability of the RemoteDesk platform components developed in Batch 17. Each risk is categorized by area, described, and assigned a severity and likelihood.
