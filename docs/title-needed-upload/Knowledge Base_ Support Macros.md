# Knowledge Base: Support Macros

This document provides a collection of standardized support macros (pre-written responses or templates) for common inquiries and issues encountered by RemoteDesk users. These macros aim to streamline support operations, ensure consistent communication, and provide quick, accurate responses.

## General Inquiries

### 1. Welcome and Initial Acknowledgment

**Macro Name:** `Welcome_Acknowledgment`

**Usage:** Use as the first response to a new support ticket.

```
Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for reaching out to RemoteDesk Support. We've received your request and are here to help. Please bear with us as we review your issue.

We aim to respond to all inquiries within [X] hours. In the meantime, you might find a solution in our Knowledge Base: [Link to RemoteDesk Knowledge Base]

Best regards,
The RemoteDesk Support Team
```

### 2. Request for More Information

**Macro Name:** `Request_More_Info`

**Usage:** When the initial ticket lacks sufficient detail to diagnose the problem.

```
Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for contacting us. To help us better understand and resolve your issue, could you please provide the following details?

*   Your RemoteDesk ID and the ID of the remote device you are trying to connect to.
*   The exact date and time (including timezone) when the issue occurred.
*   The operating systems (Windows, macOS, Linux) and their versions for both your device and the remote device.
*   Any specific error messages or codes you encountered.
*   A detailed description of the steps you took leading up to the issue.
*   (Optional) Screenshots or a short video demonstrating the problem.

This information will greatly assist us in our investigation. Thank you for your cooperation!

Best regards,
The RemoteDesk Support Team
```

### 3. Issue Resolved / Closing Ticket

**Macro Name:** `Issue_Resolved_Close_Ticket`

**Usage:** When the issue has been resolved and the ticket is being closed.

```
Hi [Customer Name],

We are pleased to inform you that your issue regarding [briefly describe issue] has been resolved. [Briefly explain resolution or refer to previous communication].

We will now close this support ticket. If you encounter any further issues or have additional questions, please don't hesitate to open a new ticket.

Thank you for your patience and understanding.

Best regards,
The RemoteDesk Support Team
```
