# Billing Settings

## View Your Plan
Go to Settings > Billing to see your current plan.

### Available Plans
| Plan | Devices | Sessions | Features | Price |
|------|---------|----------|----------|-------|
| Free | 2 | 1 | Desktop, Clipboard | $0 |
| Starter | 10 | 3 | + File Transfer | $9/mo |
| Professional | 50 | 10 | + Chat, Groups | $29/mo |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | Unlimited | All features | Contact us |

## Change Plan
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select new plan
4. Enter payment details
5. Confirm change

## Cancel Subscription
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Complete exit survey
4. Access continues until period end

## Payment Methods
- Add/remove credit cards
- Update billing address
- View payment history
- Download invoices
