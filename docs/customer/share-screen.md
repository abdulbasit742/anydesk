# Share Your Screen

## Prerequisites
- Desktop client installed and running
- Signed in to your account
- Stable internet connection

## How to Share
1. Open the RemoteDesk desktop app
2. Your 9-digit RemoteDesk ID is displayed prominently
3. Share this ID with the person who wants to connect
4. Wait for them to initiate the connection
5. When the request dialog appears, click **Accept**

## During the Session
- Your screen is transmitted to the viewer
- A green border appears around shared screen (host)
- Session toolbar appears (can be hidden)
- Viewers can see your screen but cannot control it (by default)

## Granting Control
If you want to allow remote control:
1. Click the **Remote Control** button in the toolbar
2. Toggle **Allow Control** to ON
3. The viewer can now control your mouse and keyboard

## Ending the Session
- Click **Stop Sharing** in the toolbar
- Or the viewer can click **Disconnect**
- Session ends immediately

## Tips
- Close sensitive documents before sharing
- Use **Presentation Mode** to share a specific window
- **Pause Sharing** to freeze the view temporarily
