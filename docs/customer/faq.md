# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is RemoteDesk?
RemoteDesk is a secure remote desktop application that lets you access and control computers remotely.

### Is RemoteDesk free?
Yes! We have a free plan with 2 devices and 1 concurrent session. Paid plans offer more features.

### What platforms are supported?
Windows 10+, macOS 12+, Ubuntu 20.04+, and any modern web browser.

## Security

### Is my data encrypted?
Yes. All sessions use end-to-end encryption via WebRTC DTLS/SRTP. We cannot see your screen or data.

### Can someone access my computer without permission?
No. All connections require explicit acceptance. You must approve every incoming request.

### Is RemoteDesk HIPAA/SOC2 compliant?
Enterprise plans include compliance features. Contact sales for compliance documentation.

## Technical

### What internet speed do I need?
Minimum 1 Mbps upload and download. 5+ Mbps recommended for best experience.

### Does RemoteDesk work through firewalls?
Yes. We use STUN/TURN servers to establish connections through NAT and firewalls.

### Can I use RemoteDesk on multiple monitors?
Yes. The viewer sees all monitors. You can select which monitor to share.

## Billing

### How do I cancel?
Go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.

### Can I get a refund?
Contact support within 7 days of purchase for refund consideration.

### Do you offer annual billing?
Yes. Annual billing provides a 20% discount.

## Enterprise

### Do you offer SSO?
Yes. Enterprise plans support SAML 2.0 and OIDC.

### Can I self-host?
Contact sales for on-premises deployment options.

### Do you have an API?
Yes. Enterprise plans include API access. See our developer docs.
