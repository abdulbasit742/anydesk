# RemoteDesk Billing Guide

## Plans

### Free
- 1 device
- Basic support
- Community forums

### Starter ($9/mo)
- 5 devices
- Email support
- File transfer
- 30-day session history

### Professional ($29/mo)
- Unlimited devices
- Priority support
- Session recording
- SSO integration
- 90-day session history

### Enterprise (Custom)
- Everything in Professional
- Dedicated support
- Custom SLA
- On-premises option
- Unlimited history

## Managing Your Subscription
### Upgrade
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Select new plan
3. Confirm payment
4. Changes apply immediately

### Downgrade
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Select lower plan
3. Review feature loss
4. Confirm at period end

### Cancel
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Access continues until period end

## Payment
- Credit/debit cards accepted
- Bank transfer for Enterprise
- Invoiced annually or monthly
- Failed payments retry automatically

## Past Due
- 14-day grace period
- Warnings at 7 and 10 days
- Service restricted after 10 days
- Account suspended after 14 days

## Refunds
- Pro-rated refunds available
- Contact support within 30 days
- Enterprise: per contract terms

## Invoice Questions
- Download invoices from Billing page
- Update billing email in settings
- Contact support for discrepancies
