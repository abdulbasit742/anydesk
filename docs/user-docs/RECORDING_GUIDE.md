# Session Recording Guide

## Overview
Session recording allows you to record remote sessions for later review.

## Requirements
- Professional or Enterprise plan
- Both parties must consent
- Sufficient storage quota

## How to Record
1. Start a session
2. Click the record button
3. Both parties receive a consent prompt
4. Recording starts when both accept
5. Stop recording anytime

## Recording Indicator
A red "REC" badge appears when recording is active.

## Playback
1. Go to Sessions > Recordings
2. Find your recording
3. Click Play or Download

## Retention
- Professional: 30 days
- Enterprise: Custom (up to 365 days)
- Automatic deletion after retention

## Privacy
- Recording requires explicit consent
- Indicator visible during recording
- Cannot record without consent
- Watermarked for security

## Download
- WebM format (VP9 codec)
- MP4 available on Enterprise
- Encrypted download link
- Expires after 24 hours
