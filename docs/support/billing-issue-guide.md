# Troubleshooting Billing Issues

## Cannot Update Payment Method
1. Go to Settings -> Billing
2. Click "Update Payment Method"
3. Enter new card details
4. Ensure card is not expired
5. Try different browser if fails

## Invoice Questions
- Invoices emailed to billing contact
- Download from Settings -> Billing -> Invoices
- VAT/GST number can be added
- PO numbers supported on enterprise plans

## Refund Requests
- Pro-rated refunds within 30 days of charge
- Annual plans: refund unused months
- Contact billing@remotedesk.io
- Processing time: 5-10 business days

## Seat Changes
- Add seats: immediate charge for prorated amount
- Remove seats: credit applied to next invoice
- Minimum 1 seat on all paid plans

## Failed Payments
1. Card expired -> Update payment method
2. Insufficient funds -> Add funds, retry
3. Bank declined -> Contact bank
4. After 3 failures: account restricted
5. Grace period: 7 days before restriction

## Enterprise Billing
- Annual invoicing available
- Wire transfer accepted
- Custom terms available
- Dedicated account manager
