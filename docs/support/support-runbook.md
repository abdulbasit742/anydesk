# Support Runbook

## Operating Hours
- L1: 24/7 (email/chat)
- L2: Business hours (engineering)
- L3: On-call (critical issues)

## Ticket Triage
1. **Receive**: Ticket enters queue
2. **Classify**: Bug, Question, Feature, Critical
3. **Prioritize**: P0-P4
4. **Assign**: To appropriate team member
5. **Resolve**: Work toward resolution
6. **Close**: Confirm resolution with customer

## Priority Definitions
| Priority | Response | Resolution | Example |
|----------|----------|------------|---------|
| P0 | 15 min | 4 hours | Service down |
| P1 | 1 hour | 24 hours | Major feature broken |
| P2 | 4 hours | 3 days | Minor feature issue |
| P3 | 24 hours | 1 week | Question, workaround exists |
| P4 | 48 hours | Next sprint | Feature request |

## Communication Standards
- Acknowledge within SLA
- Update every 24h until resolved
- Use customer-friendly language
- Provide workarounds when available
- Escalate early if stuck

## Tools
- Ticketing: Internal dashboard
- Knowledge base: docs/support/
- Escalation: Slack #support-escalation
