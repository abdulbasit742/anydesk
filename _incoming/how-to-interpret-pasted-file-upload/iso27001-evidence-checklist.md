# RemoteDesk ISO 27001 Evidence Checklist

## Introduction
This checklist outlines the evidence required for achieving and maintaining ISO/IEC 27001 certification for RemoteDesk. ISO 27001 is an international standard for information security management systems (ISMS). This document helps in preparing for the certification audit by listing key artifacts and documentation aligned with the standard's requirements and Annex A controls.

## ISO 27001 Requirements (Clauses 4-10)

### 1. Context of the Organization (Clause 4)
- [ ] **Scope of ISMS:** Document defining the boundaries and applicability of the ISMS.
- [ ] **Interested Parties Analysis:** Identification of internal and external interested parties and their requirements.
- [ ] **Internal and External Issues:** Documentation of issues that can affect the ISMS.

### 2. Leadership (Clause 5)
- [ ] **Information Security Policy:** Top management commitment and policy statement.
- [ ] **Roles, Responsibilities, and Authorities:** Documented information security roles and responsibilities.

### 3. Planning (Clause 6)
- [ ] **Risk Assessment and Treatment Process:** Documented methodology for information security risk assessment and treatment.
- [ ] **Information Security Risk Assessment Report:** Results of risk assessments.
- [ ] **Information Security Risk Treatment Plan:** Plan for addressing identified risks.
- [ ] **Statement of Applicability (SoA):** Document justifying the inclusion/exclusion of Annex A controls.
- [ ] **Information Security Objectives:** Documented information security objectives and plans to achieve them.

### 4. Support (Clause 7)
- [ ] **Resources:** Evidence of adequate resources (human, infrastructure, environment) for the ISMS.
- [ ] **Competence Records:** Records of employee competence, training, skills, experience.
- [ ] **Awareness Training Records:** Records of information security awareness training.
- [ ] **Communication Plan:** Documented plan for internal and external communications relevant to the ISMS.
- [ ] **Documented Information Control:** Procedures for creating, updating, and controlling documented information.

### 5. Operation (Clause 8)
- [ ] **Operational Planning and Control:** Evidence of planning, implementation, and control of processes to meet information security requirements.
- [ ] **Information Security Risk Treatment Implementation:** Evidence of implementing the risk treatment plan.

### 6. Performance Evaluation (Clause 9)
- [ ] **Monitoring, Measurement, Analysis, and Evaluation:** Procedures and records for monitoring and measuring ISMS performance.
- [ ] **Internal Audit Program and Reports:** Schedule and results of internal ISMS audits.
- [ ] **Management Review Records:** Records of management reviews of the ISMS.

### 7. Improvement (Clause 10)
- [ ] **Nonconformity and Corrective Action Records:** Records of nonconformities and corrective actions taken.
- [ ] **Continual Improvement Evidence:** Evidence of continual improvement of the ISMS.

## Annex A Controls (Selected Examples)

### A.5 Information Security Policies
- [ ] **A.5.1.1 Policies for Information Security:** Documented information security policies.
- [ ] **A.5.1.2 Review of the Policies for Information Security:** Records of policy reviews.

### A.6 Organization of Information Security
- [ ] **A.6.1.1 Information Security Roles and Responsibilities:** Defined and communicated roles.
- [ ] **A.6.1.2 Segregation of Duties:** Evidence of segregation of duties.
- [ ] **A.6.2.2 Mobile Device Policy:** Policy for mobile devices.

### A.7 Human Resource Security
- [ ] **A.7.1.1 Screening:** Background checks.
- [ ] **A.7.2.2 Information Security Awareness, Education and Training:** Training records.
- [ ] **A.7.3.1 Termination or Change of Employment Responsibilities:** Offboarding procedures.

### A.8 Asset Management
- [ ] **A.8.1.1 Inventory of Assets:** List of information assets.
- [ ] **A.8.2.1 Classification of Information:** Information classification scheme.
- [ ] **A.8.3.1 Management of Removable Media:** Policy for removable media.

### A.9 Access Control
- [ ] **A.9.2.1 User Registration and De-registration:** Procedures for managing user access.
- [ ] **A.9.2.3 Management of Privileged Access Rights:** Control over privileged accounts.
- [ ] **A.9.4.1 Access Control Policy:** Policy for access control.

### A.10 Cryptography
- [ ] **A.10.1.1 Policy on the Use of Cryptographic Controls:** Policy for encryption.

### A.11 Physical and Environmental Security
- [ ] **A.11.1.2 Physical Entry Controls:** Physical access logs.
- [ ] **A.11.2.1 Equipment Siting and Protection:** Secure placement of equipment.

### A.12 Operations Security
- [ ] **A.12.1.1 Documented Operating Procedures:** Operational procedures.
- [ ] **A.12.2.1 Controls Against Malware:** Anti-malware controls.
- [ ] **A.12.4.1 Event Logging:** System and security logs.
- [ ] **A.12.4.3 Administrator and Operator Logs:** Logs of administrative activities.
- [ ] **A.12.6.1 Management of Technical Vulnerabilities:** Vulnerability management process.

### A.13 Communications Security
- [ ] **A.13.1.1 Network Security Management:** Network security controls.
- [ ] **A.13.2.1 Information Transfer Policies and Procedures:** Policies for information transfer.

### A.14 System Acquisition, Development and Maintenance
- [ ] **A.14.2.5 Secure System Engineering Principles:** Secure development lifecycle.
- [ ] **A.14.2.8 System Security Testing:** Security testing records.

### A.15 Supplier Relationships
- [ ] **A.15.1.2 Addressing Security Within Supplier Agreements:** Security clauses in contracts.

### A.16 Information Security Incident Management
- [ ] **A.16.1.2 Reporting Information Security Events:** Incident reporting procedures.
- [ ] **A.16.1.5 Response to Information Security Incidents:** Incident response records.

### A.17 Information Security Aspects of Business Continuity Management
- [ ] **A.17.1.2 Implementing Information Security Continuity:** Business continuity plans.

### A.18 Compliance
- [ ] **A.18.1.1 Identification of Applicable Legislation and Contractual Requirements:** Legal and regulatory compliance documentation.
- [ ] **A.18.2.1 Independent Review of Information Security:** Records of independent security reviews.
