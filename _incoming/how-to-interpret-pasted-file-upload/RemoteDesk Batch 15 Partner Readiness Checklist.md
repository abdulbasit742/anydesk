# RemoteDesk Batch 15 Partner Readiness Checklist

## Introduction
This checklist outlines the key requirements and steps for ensuring partner readiness for RemoteDesk, focusing on the artifacts and processes developed in Batch 15. It aims to provide a structured approach for onboarding and supporting partners in selling and integrating with RemoteDesk.

## Checklist Items

### 1. Partner Onboarding and Management
- [ ] Partner onboarding guide is finalized and reviewed.
- [ ] Partner account DTOs are implemented and validated.
- [ ] Partner program tiers are clearly defined and documented.
- [ ] Partner portal access and registration process are ready.

### 2. Integration and Marketplace
- [ ] Marketplace application and listing DTOs are finalized.
- [ ] Marketplace integration documentation is comprehensive and up-to-date.
- [ ] Marketplace UI components are functional and intuitive.
- [ ] API productization strategy is documented and communicated.
- [ ] API key management and usage tracking are implemented.

### 3. Support and Enablement
- [ ] Partner-specific support channels and processes are established.
- [ ] Sales and marketing collateral for partners is ready.
- [ ] Technical training and certification programs are defined.

### 4. Legal and Compliance
- [ ] Partner agreement templates are finalized and reviewed.
- [ ] Revenue sharing and commission models are clearly defined.
- [ ] Data processing agreements for partners are in place.

### 5. Communication and Launch
- [ ] Partner launch communication plan is ready.
- [ ] Dedicated partner manager(s) assigned and trained.
- [ ] Partner feedback mechanism is established.

## Final Sign-off
- [ ] All checklist items completed and verified.
- [ ] Stakeholder approval for partner program launch.
- [ ] Initial partner onboarding dates confirmed.
